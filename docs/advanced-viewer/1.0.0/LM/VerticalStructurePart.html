VerticalStructurePart

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:Common:Geospatial:VerticalStructurePart

A part of the vertical structure that can be represented as point, line or polygon with vertical extent.

Properties Definition Type
lighting An element of the vertical structure part lighting. LightElement
visibleMaterial An indicator of the type(s) of material that compose the load-bearing structure and/or exterior facing of a vertical construction. CodeVerticalStructureMaterialType
isMobile Indication whether the vertical structure part is expected to move around its nominal location. Boolean
verticalExtent The vertical extent of the vertical structure part. ValDistanceType
type An indicator of the type of vertical structure part. CodeVerticalStructureType
topElevation The vertical distance of the highest point of the vertical structure measured from mean sea level. ValAltitudeType
markingSecondColour The secondary colour for the marking of the vertical structure part. CodeColourType
markingPattern The general layout of the external paint or another marking element, intended to increase the visibility of the vertical structure part. CodeVerticalStructureMarkingType
markingFirstColour The principal colour of the marking of the vertical structure part. CodeColourType
verticalExtentAccuracy Accuracy of the value of the declared vertical extent of the vertical structure part. ValDistanceType
isFrangible An indicator that the vertical structure part is easily broken. Boolean
designator A distinguishing label by which the vertical structure part is identified locally. CharacterString
constructionStatus The status of construction of the man-made object that constitutes the vertical structure part. CodeStatusConstructionType
