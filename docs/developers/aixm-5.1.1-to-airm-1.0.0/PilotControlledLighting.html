PilotControlledLighting

PilotControlledLighting.activatedGroundLighting

The lighting system on the ground that may be remotely activated by the pilot.

Properties Definition Type
intensitySteps The number of intensity steps/levels for a particular type of lighting system.
standByIntensity The intensity step/level of the lighting system when not in use.
activatedGroundLighting The lighting system on the ground that may be remotely activated by the pilot.
controlledLightIntensity The characteristics of the lighting system (intensity, number of clicks required to activate/deactivate the system).
annotation
type Type of pilot controlled lighting service - standard or non-standard.
radioFrequency The radio frequency to be used by the pilot for the activation of the system.
duration The length of time the lighting is illuminated, normally 15 minutes.
activationInstruction Operational instructions for controlling the lighting systems.
missing data Service providing airborne control of lights by keying the aircraft's microphone. Often available at locations without specified hours for lighting and where there is no control tower or Flight Service Station (FSS); or when the tower or FSS is closed (locations with part-time tower or FSS).
