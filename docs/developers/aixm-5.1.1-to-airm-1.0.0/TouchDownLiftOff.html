Surface characteristics of the touch down lift off zone.

Properties Definition Type
surfaceProperties Surface characteristics of the touch down lift off zone.
missing data A load bearing area on which a helicopter may touch down or lift-off.
availability The operational status of the TouchDownLiftOff area.
abandoned An indicator that the touch down and lift-off area is no longer in operational use, but it is still physically present and visible, although usually in a degraded state.
designator The designator of the touch down and lift-off area.
helicopterClass The class of a helicopter (based on its performance during a critical power unit failure after take-off) associated with a touch down and lift-off area.
length The value of the physical length of the touchdown and lift-off area.
slope The slope (rate of upward inclination of the surface from the horizontal) of the surface of a touch down and lift-off area.
width The value of the physical width of the touchdown and lift-off area.
extent Extent of the TouchDownLiftOff zone.
approachTakeOffArea The approach and take off runway area containing the touchdown lift off zone.

contaminant
