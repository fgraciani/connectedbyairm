Runway

Runway.surfaceProperties

Surface characteristics of the runway.

Properties Definition Type
widthShoulder The runway shoulder width.
widthAccuracy Accuracy of the value of the physical width of the runway.
abandoned An indicator that the surface is no longer in operational use, but is still physically present and visible, although usually in a degraded state.
designator The designator of a runway, used to uniquely identify it at an aerodrome/heliport.
missing data A defined rectangular area on a land aerodrome/heliport prepared for the landing and take-off of aircraft. Note: this includes the concept of Final Approach and Take-Off Area (FATO) for helicopters.
lengthAccuracy Accuracy of the value of the physical length of the runway.
lengthOffset A value specifying the longitudinal offset of the strip, when it is not symmetrically extended beyond the two runway ends. Where a strip is a defined area including the runway and, if applicable, the stopway.
lengthStrip The physical length of the strip. Where a strip is a defined area including the runway and, if applicable, the stopway.
nominalLength The declared longitudinal extent of the runway for operational (performance) calculations.
nominalWidth The declared transversal extent of the runway for operational (performance) calculations.
type The type can be either runway for airplanes or Final Approach and Take-off Area (FATO) for helicopters.
associatedAirportHeliport The Airport the Runway is associated with.
widthOffset A value specifying the lateral offset of the strip, when it is not symmetrically extended beyond the two runway edges. Where a strip is a defined area including the runway and, if applicable, the stopway.
widthStrip The physical width of the strip. Where a strip is a defined area including the runway and, if applicable, the stopway.
areaContaminant Area contamination of a runway.
overallContaminant Runway contaminant.
annotation
surfaceProperties Surface characteristics of the runway.
