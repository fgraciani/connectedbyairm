atcUnitNameOrAlternate

fb:AtcUnitReferenceType:atcUnitNameOrAlternate

The full textual name of the ATC unit [adapted from AIXM 5.1] or an alternate identifier for the unit. [FIXM]

Type: TextNameType

Semantic Correspondence
AIRM Concept Definition
Unit@name The full textual name of a unit, established according to the rules specified by International Civil Aviation Organisation (ICAO), and specifically in the official language of the country, transposed into the Latin Alphabet where necessary.
Unit@designator A distinguishing label, term, abbreviation or acronym used to identify the Unit.
